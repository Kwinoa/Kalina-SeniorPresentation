A Little Bit About Myself...

Why a Website?

Though I realize that a website may not be the best way to present about myself to an audience, I kind of got bored of slides after making dozens of them over the past 4 years. I also wanted to do something challenging for myself, as my ability would be the only thing limiting my presentation. Another plus about websites is that they are customizable and flexible to a certain extent. Finally, web developing was a possible career path that I had in mind.
