4 Years...

14-year-old

At the start I kind of felt out of place and had many worries and anxieties...
- I was naively excited to leave the school I attended for 9 years
- I was under a lot of pressure to choose a major and plan out my career path early.
- I had low confidence in my ability to perform well in my classes.
- I genuinely doubted that I would graduate from high school.

4 years...
1466 days...
31 courses...
24 absences...
too many tests to count...
and lots of stress later...

Me at 18 years old

Now, in terms of my attitude rather than actual progress...
- I don't stress about having a full career path planned out already.
- I can take any class and feel confident that I will, one way or another, pass it.
- I know that I am graduating.
